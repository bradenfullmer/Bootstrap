A Blacksmith's Journey

A Blacksmith's Journey



INTERACTIVE_COMIC_BASE



This game was a group project from GIMM 250. It is an interactive comic, where the player is being told a story, and has the ability to interact with it. For this game, we decided to give the player choices, which not only give you a different ending, but in some cases change the world around you! We also made it so you can pick up items as well to help you with your choices throughout the game. So please, try your hand at A Blacksmith's Journey. Which ending will you get?
