Games and Art



On this page you can see the artwork I have done in a few of my GIMM classes, along with some screenshots of some games I made outside of GIMM. The reason I decided to put photos of games here, is because I want to show them, but keep the games section for games that can be played. If you would like to download some of the games shown, please go check my other site and my itch.io page. So enjoy, I hope you like what you see!

This was a big project for me early on in GIMM, the post-apocolyptic background painting. We had to take a photo of someplace in downtown Boise and then transform it into an apocolyptic setting. It was a very difficult project for me, since I don't feel the most confident in my art skills. However in the end, I think it turned oout much better than I would've thought. This project taught me a lot on how to use photoshop in a much more effective manner.
